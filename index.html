PANAMA WILT OF BANANA

INTRODUCTION

Panama disease , a devasting disease of banana caused by the soil inhabiting species

Fusarium oxysporum forma specialis cubense.

Symptoms

Yellowing of lower or older leaves caused by the fungus blocking the water conducting tissue within the banana plant stem. Lower leaves collapsing to form a 'skirt' around the plant, cut open, the stem is discoloured from yellow to red, through dard brown or black.

MANAGEMENT

Chemical control

  • Fungicide dipping forroots before planting.
    • Stem injections Surface sterilants

    Cultural control

  • Intercropping with Chinese chive
    • Biological control
  • Using antagonistic microorganisms likePseudomonas fluorescens, Bacillus subtilis Resistant cultivars Gros Michel, Silk, Pome, Pisang Awak, Cavendish Visit for further information
